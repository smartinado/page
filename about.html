Tasmagic is crafted by Kamal Farai

Kamal is a longtime Tasmota builder who needed faster provisioning and safer automation reviews. The internal toolkit he started in Flutter grew into the Tasmagic app—an offline-first control room with multilingual UI, 60+ device templates, Berry automation tooling, and deep analytics. Every release still comes directly from him.

Mission

A single control room

Replace scattered scripts with one interface covering discovery, provisioning, automation, analytics, and maintenance.

Principles

Offline-first & transparent

No hidden infrastructure. Automations are readable Berry code, dashboards are local, and data remains on your network.

Technology

Flutter + pure Dart

One codebase powers Android today and desktop builds tomorrow, keeping updates fast for a solo maintainer.

Product milestones

Discovery 2.0

Adaptive subnet scans, MQTT health probes, and instant dashboard generation.

Automation Studio

Round-trip Berry parsing with trigger and condition diffing plus visual previews.

Provisioning Suite

Captive portal assistant, Wi-Fi templates, and credential vaulting with local storage.

Insights & Backups

Historical sensor charts, log streaming, and encrypted device or automation backups.
