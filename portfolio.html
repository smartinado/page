Tasmagic capability map

Every screen in the app is designed to reduce onsite time. Here is a quick tour of the flagship modules.

Discovery

Adaptive network scanner

Auto-detects subnets, filters Tasmota fingerprints, and surfaces health status. Generates device cards with actionable shortcuts.

Dashboards

Auto layout engine

Builds control rooms for relays, dimmers, RGB/CT lights, and sensors. Drag-and-drop sections and ship custom branding for clients.

Automation

Berry visualizer

Upload Berry scripts from devices, inspect triggers, tweak logic, and safely redeploy. Includes syntax validation and version history.

Templates

Curated hardware catalog

Browse 60+ module definitions, preview GPIO mappings, and push presets or JSON templates directly to the device console.

Provisioning

Guided Wi-Fi flows

Captive portal assistant, credential vault, and automatic MQTT staging with validation of telemetry topics.

Insights

Sensor analytics

Overlay charts for temperature, humidity, power, PM2.5, CO₂, and custom metrics. Export CSV snapshots for compliance.
