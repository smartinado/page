All your Tasmota devices, orchestrated from one focused app.

Tasmagic is an independent Flutter application handcrafted by Kamal Farai. It bundles discovery, Wi-Fi onboarding, automation, dashboards, and analytics so installers and power users can stay offline, private, and in control—no external services, no build pipelines, and no hidden team.

What makes Tasmagic different?

Built for professional installers, managed service providers, and in-house operations teams who depend on Tasmota daily.

Device intelligence

Instant discovery

Scan the network, fingerprint firmware, and auto-generate dashboards for every relay, light, or sensor in seconds.

Provisioning

Guided Wi-Fi onboarding

Batch provision captive portals, push MQTT credentials, and validate relay states before leaving the site.

Automation

Visual Berry builder

Translate Berry code back into editable triggers, conditions, and actions. Deploy automations without syntax anxiety.

Operations

Live dashboards

Rich charts for sensors, relay overrides, RGB/CT lighting controls, template switching, and OTA firmware management.
