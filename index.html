Hand-built by Kamal Farai

I built Tasmagic so the Tasmota community gets a user-friendly control room.

Tasmota is a legendary open-source firmware with a huge maker community, but day-to-day provisioning can still feel like a pile of scripts. I'm Kamal Farai, and Tasmagic is my Flutter app that bundles discovery, Wi-Fi onboarding, Berry automation, dashboards, and analytics into one private workflow—no external services, no hidden team, just me shipping the app I use.

Why I keep iterating on Tasmagic

I maintain Tasmagic for installers, MSPs, and hobbyists who depend on open-source Tasmota firmware every day but still want a calm, user-friendly interface.

Device intelligence

Instant discovery

Scan the network, fingerprint firmware, and auto-generate dashboards for every relay, light, or sensor in seconds.

Provisioning

Guided Wi-Fi onboarding

Batch provision captive portals, push MQTT credentials, and validate relay states before leaving the site.

Automation

Visual Berry builder

Translate Berry code back into editable triggers, conditions, and actions. Deploy automations without syntax anxiety.

Operations

Live dashboards

Rich charts for sensors, relay overrides, RGB/CT lighting controls, template switching, and OTA firmware management.
