I built Tasmagic so the Tasmota community can onboard devices without friction.

Tasmota is a legendary open-source firmware with a huge community. Tasmagic is my take on a commercial-grade companion app: a fully offline Flutter experience that unifies discovery, provisioning, dashboards, Berry automation, and analytics. The downloadable build on this site is free forever—it is how I learn what the community needs while the Premium tier is polished for release soon.

Download Free Community Build See upcoming tiers

No telemetry, no account setup, and the APK is signed by me.

What the free build includes

  • Dual-mode device discovery (quick scan + full subnet)
  • Guided Wi-Fi onboarding with credential vault
  • Instant dashboards for relays, lights, sensors, and energy devices
  • Visual Berry automation editor with round-trip parsing
  • Sensor history charts with CSV export
  • Template catalog covering 60+ popular devices

What is coming next

Premium and Pro are in development and will unlock appearance editing, automation diffing, ops reports, and fleet-level backups. These tiers are packaged by me and shipped the same way—signed APKs with zero extra build steps.
