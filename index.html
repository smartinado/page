What makes Tasmagic different?

Purpose-built tooling for installers, makers, and operations teams who rely on Tasmota every day.

Device intelligence

Instant discovery

Scan the network, fingerprint firmware, and auto-generate dashboards for every relay, light, or sensor in seconds.

Provisioning

Guided Wi-Fi onboarding

Batch provision captive portals, push MQTT credentials, and validate relay states before leaving the site.

Automation

Visual Berry builder

Translate Berry code back into editable triggers, conditions, and actions. Deploy automations without syntax anxiety.

Operations

Live dashboards

Rich charts for sensors, relay overrides, RGB/CT lighting controls, template switching, and OTA firmware management.

Variants tuned for every deployment
