A guided walkthrough

I designed every screen to feel like a professional tool, yet approachable for the broader Tasmota community. Below is the flow I use when commissioning new installs.

1 · Scan & fingerprint

Choose Quick Scan for instant subnet sweeps or Deep Scan for exhaustive checks. Devices are fingerprinted with module, firmware, and health hints so you know what you are about to provision.

2 · Wi-Fi onboarding

Guided Wi-Fi setup validates credentials, configures MQTT topics, and saves templates for reuse. Nothing leaves your network.

3 · Dashboard generation

Dashboards are created automatically based on the relays, dimmers, RGB lights, and sensors reported by the device. You can still rearrange cards to match your workflow.

4 · Automate visually

The Berry studio turns triggers, conditions, and actions into readable code on the fly. You can import existing Berry scripts from devices, inspect them, tweak, and redeploy with confidence.

5 · Monitor & learn

Sensor analytics provide min/max/average insights with CSV exports. It is the fastest way to validate energy usage or climate tuning without remote dashboards.
